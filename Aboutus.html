Who we are:

Golchha organization is one of the leading private sector enterprise in Nepal with over 100 years' of legacy of trust, reliability and delivery of promise. The organization with many flagship brands under its name has forayed into the education sector under Hulas Education, and neosphere is its recent conception in that front. Promoted by Golchha Organization, neosphere has been conceived with the objective of developing professional expertise and skilled manpower in IT. This high-end institute has been launched in collaboration with Aptech, India with a vision to create relevent IT education in Nepal. And aims to provide a practical learning environment for students and working professionals who are willing to explore and build their career in IT Sector. The establishment of neosphere is a major step towards harnessing multi- dimensional facets of IT at all levels.The institute is envisaged to be a well equipped one with the expert faculty. This training hub delivers a diverse range of learning and skill development programs to the individual and corporate learners in various areas including Software Development, Database Administration, Hardware and Networking, Server Administration, Digital Media Marketing, Graphic Designing, Multimedia and Animation, Project Management, Cyber Security, Ethical Hacking and others.. The course offerings include industry- endorsed and a set of career- oriented programs for students and professionals from different streams. Having partnered with the leading educators and industry experts like Aptech, Adobe, Esri, SAP, Dell, Certiport at neosphere. We are committed to provide immersive learning experiences which seek to equip our students with the necessary knowledge and skills to be competitive and effective contributors within the digital industry.
